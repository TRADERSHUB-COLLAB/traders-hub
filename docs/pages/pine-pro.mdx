# Pine Pro Access

Pine Pro is the premium tier of TradersHub indicators built natively for TradingView. It is bundled with the **Ultra** plan and unlocks the full suite of multi-market BUY/SELL strategies powered by the TradersHub API.

## What you get

- **Full indicator library** — every published Pine Pro script across US Stocks, Crypto, Forex, JSE, LSE, Indices, and Commodities.
- **Live API-linked signals** — indicators read from the same engine that powers `/signals` on the API.
- **All endpoints, all markets** — no per-market gating; one key unlocks everything.
- **Priority updates** — new strategies and refinements ship to Ultra users first.
- **Invite-only TradingView access** — scripts are added to your TradingView username after verification.

## How access works

1. Subscribe to the **Ultra** plan on RapidAPI or via the TradersHub site.
2. Your API key is automatically flagged with `pine_pro_access: true` at the gateway.
3. Submit your TradingView username through the access form linked in your welcome email.
4. Scripts are granted to your TradingView account, typically within one business day.

## Verifying your access

Call the gateway with your Ultra key:

```bash
curl -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_ULTRA_KEY" \
  https://api.tradershub.dev/v1/account/entitlements
```

A response containing `"pine_pro_access": true` confirms your entitlement is live.

## FAQ

**Can I get Pine Pro without the API?**
Pine Pro is bundled with the Ultra plan and is not sold separately. The plan includes the full API quota as well.

**Do indicators work on the free TradingView tier?**
Yes. A free TradingView account is sufficient to receive and run Pine Pro scripts.

**What happens if I downgrade?**
Indicator access is revoked at the end of the current billing cycle. Your API key continues to work on the lower tier’s quota.

**Can I share scripts with my team?**
Each Ultra seat maps to one TradingView username. Additional seats can be added from your account dashboard.
