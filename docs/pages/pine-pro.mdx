## How access works

1. Subscribe to the **Pine Pro** or **Ultra** plan on RapidAPI or via the TradersHub site.
2. Your API key is automatically flagged with the right entitlement at the gateway.
3. Submit your TradingView username through the access form linked in your welcome email.
4. The script is granted to your TradingView account, typically within one business day.

## Verifying your access

Call the gateway with your key:

```bash
curl -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_KEY" \
https://api.tradershub.dev/v1/account/entitlements
```

A response containing `"pine_pro_access": true` confirms your entitlement is live.

## FAQ

**Is the script open source?** No. TradersHub Pine Pro is a closed-source, protected TradingView script. You see it work on your chart; the engine stays ours.

**Does it work on the free TradingView tier?** Yes. A free TradingView account is sufficient.

**Can I use it on any symbol?** Yes — stocks, crypto, forex, JSE, LSE, indices, and gold. Anything TradingView charts, Pine Pro reads.

**What happens if I downgrade?** Indicator access is revoked at the end of the current billing cycle. Your API key continues to work on the lower tier’s quota.

**Can I share scripts with my team?** Each seat maps to one TradingView username. Add additional seats from your account dashboard.

**What’s the difference between Pine Pro and Ultra?** Pine Pro is everything you see on a single TradingView chart. Ultra adds multi-timeframe scanning, multi-symbol bulk scans, liquidity sweep detection, sentiment-weighted scoring, and JSON output — the things only an API can deliver.
